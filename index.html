How to play?
First of all , you can choose the level of hardness (it has set on medium level as default).
When you click on "play" button a bunch of cards will be shown to you for a few seconds(the time is depended on the level you have chosen).
Then you can match the two similar cards by clicking on them during a specified time.
If you match all the cards in time , you will win the game.
